You may be wondering how we will get from one destination to another throughout the island. Well, Taniti offers visitors public buses that serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses are also served throughout the island. Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport. Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors. Make sure to wear your helmet as it's required by LAW! If you're feeling adventurous, you can walk on foot.
